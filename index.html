About The Website

The Navigation Bar at the top of the website consist the function of a scrollspy, which means that, upon clicking one, the website would scroll to that specific area of the website. Furthermore, my logo is also placed there. Only two pages are not listed on the navigation bar; Namely, the About Page-this specific part-and the Portfolio, which can be found in the gallery upon clicking the ellipses at the top.

Upon hovering on the Education area or tapping if on mobile or tab, the degree and academic level are listed and aligned with their corresponding year in which I begun and finished said level. The name of the school, Holy Angel University, and the locations were also provdied, alongside the certification I attained during the year level. For the Skills area, a button and progress bar is provided. The current length of the colored area represent the average of my rating towards the competencies within said skill. The area is separated into three categories: Front-End, Back-End, Tools, Frameworks, and Additional Skills, all of which can be hovered-or clicked if on mobile or tablet-to provide more options on my self-evaluation. The Gallery contains the previews of my previous website projects throughout the years, beginning with the latest one and ending with the oldest one. Moreover, if the ellipses atop were to be clicked, you will be redirected to my Portfolio, which will provide more information about these projects, as well as other non-website projects I've made. Lastly, the footer consist of my Contacts as well as a way to submit a form to contact me!
